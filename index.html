    Collection Design

    What This Site Will House

    Released PDFs

    Direct links to declassified scans hosted by the Bush Presidential Library, National Archives, or other official releases as item-level South Asia files are identified.

    Source Series

    Series-level anchors from Richard N. Haass files in the National Security Council records, preserving NAIDs, local identifiers, FOIA tracking numbers, restrictions, and arrangement notes.

    Document Metadata

    Date, participant, country, title, release status, NAID, source collection, chapter assignment, page count, and declassification trail.

    Research Notes

    Short, separable notes for Afghanistan, Pakistan, India, regional security, nuclear policy, sanctions, Kashmir, and post-Cold War diplomatic transitions.

    Build Plan

    Ingest Checklist

    1. 01 Start from the three Haass series: Chronological Files, Meeting Files, and Presidential Meeting Files.
    2. 02 Harvest South Asia presidential meetings, teleconferences, NSC meeting folders, and release PDFs within those source anchors.
    3. 03 Assign each record to Afghanistan, Pakistan, India, or Regional.
    4. 04 Sort records chronologically within each chapter by meeting or document date.
    5. 05 Normalize countries, participants, release status, NAIDs, local identifiers, date lines, and source notes.
    6. 06 Attach official catalog links, PDF links, FOIA tracking numbers, and OCR-derived page counts for each record.
    7. 07 Track partial, denied, restricted, and newly released records as the chapter set matures.
